Projects

Dino Dice

Dino Dice is the first project I worked on during my internship at Nerdy Dragon. The goal of the project was to create a virtual version of some math exercises DMTI had created. I created this project in Construct 3 and was the sole programmer and game designer for this project. I did not create the graphics for this game but I did create the sounds that play when you get and answer right or wrong. It is now available on the Apple Store and will be available on Google Play soon.

Arcanic

Arcanic was the second game that my partner and I competed with in the SkillsUSA State and National competitions. In this game you play as Wrench an Inxo protector of ancient magical relics and while on patrol one day a villain tries to steal them! Wrench tries to fight the villain to get them back but the artifacts react and teleport them both to a previously unknown island. It's now up to Wrench to get the relics back and stop the villain before it's to late.
In this game I was the primary designer and lead a team of designers who assisted me with different aspects of the UI and character design. My partner and I worked on the actual game mechanics and concept together.
Arcanic won 1st place at the state level and then went on to get 1st place at the national level for our competition.
